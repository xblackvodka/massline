What Is Survivalism? What Is Left-Survivalism?

Survivalism is a movement of preparedness. Survivalists prepare for any kind of emergency, from social unrest to catastrophic failure. However, the survivalist movement is primarily dominated by Christians, rightists, and right-militias. This is why I seek to separate survivalism into right-survivalism and left-survivalism (and form left-militias).

I am a left-survivalist. Left-survivalism is, roughly, revolutionary socialist ideology infused in the praxis of survivalism.

My target audience is comrades of the imperialist core, but I want to expand this audience to include survivors of imperialism in the periphery zones, and comrades facing the threat of imperialist war in the near-future. It is my hope that left-survivalism serves to promote preparedness and security culture throughout the whole socialist movement, as a means of building a wider culture of resistance in the Third-Worldist sense.

Why RLSM?

RLSM aims to be a component of the Revolutionary Anti-Imperialist Movement, an organization devoted to expanding the creative application of RAIMism. RAIM encourages a great deal of creativity, integrity and discipline, and it is our belief that RLSM is a creative application well-suited for helping to build the subjective forces for revolution on the following grounds:

  • RLSM can bring comrades face-to-face and introduce them to a new and challenging environment.
  • RLSM can teach comrades basic (and advanced) guerrilla and survival techniques that one needs in order to respond to calls for armed insurrection and/or relief of local, regional and global disasters (crises of capitalist-imperialism?).
  • RLSM can build real camaraderie, discipline and organizational unity, by functioning as a strict military-style operation, communist-in-character, founded upon revolutionary anti-imperialist (and Third-Worldist) principles.
