Welcome to the chatroom. A level of Internet-based communication is productive, although I encourage you to go outside and put your boots on the ground. Feel free to discuss Maoism, Third-Worldism, and national liberation theories.
The chatroom is public. Do not reveal personal information or information about comrades or organizations.


If you’d like a principled think-tank in a longform format, check out Spectre Of Communism.

General Rules

  1. Use gender-neutral pronouns (“one”, “they”, “their”, “folks”) , or ask for pronouns. Dysphoria is painful.
  2. If you don’t know the answer to a question, make an investigation, or you have no right to speak.
  3. Don’t sling mud at others over non-antagonistic contradictions.
  4. When proven wrong, don’t backpeddle! Self-criticize.
  5. Listen to others! It’s a part of solidarity.
