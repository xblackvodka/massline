MONDAY, MAY 19th, 2014

Statement | On The Pyongyang Apartment Collapse

WRITTEN by FENG HUANG aka IRON PHOENIX

On May 13th, 2014, an apartment building collapsed in Phyongchon district, Pyongyang, crushing hundreds to death, bereaving hundreds of Korean families.

Firstly, let me offer my deepest and most heartfelt condolences to all Koreans in their time of mourning and loss. Secondly, let me say this: Forgiveness is deserved by any entity, if it makes an apology, makes reparations, and works to correct its behaviour. If the NK government launches an honest national inquiry into safety and design regulations regarding the construction and upkeep of residential property, and works to correct its errors, let it be forgiven.

The DPRK reported this via KCNA, taking responsibility for the collapse, and offering its “profound apologies and consolation”. Since then, I’ve seen Jucheists defend the NK government by arguing that when bridges collapse in AmeriKKKa, it is no fault of the government, so why criticize the government of North Korea for an apartment collapse? Fortunately, their fallacy in logic is clear: the government of AmeriKKKa is at fault for the collapse of bridges, because the regulation of bridge construction is overseen by the government at the federal level. And it is not as if buildings collapse because of missing nails, no –  buildings collapse due to structural design flaws, which a WPK officer acknowledged in a public statement issued to grieving families, widows and orphans.

The response to the KCNA report by the largely Internet-based First-Worldist “movement” is appaling, but not shockingly so. After all, the people here absolving the NK government of all responsibility for this fatal mismanagement are the same people who champion to defend the Kim dynasty for all of its anti-communist practices. It was only last month that KCNA expressed disgust for homosexuality in one of its news reports, the tank-Jucheists shouting down the unbelievers as “bad Marxists” and “racists”. At every possible opportunity, the tank-Jucheist asks, “how can DPRK be so bad, when they love their dear leaders so”?

This is backwards, non-Marxist, and metaphysical thinking, a cage of the mind that prevents people from perceiving reality. It is a real roadblock to real revolutionary work.

Oppose DPRK worship and metaphysical Marxism!

Down with the sectarianism of the dogmato-revisionist First-Worldist Juche clique!

Long live the beautiful, Korean people!
