The following is a list of organizations, websites, political and economic lines, and leftist journals I support.

  • Indian People’s War, Filipino People’s War, Nepali People’s War - Global People’s War
  • MIM Down: An informal group that furthered the theoretical work of the MIM.
  • Maoist Internationalist Ministry of Prisons: An organization serving the masses of the prison-industrial complex.
  • International League Of People’s Struggle: Anti-imperialist league serving the anti-imperialist struggle.
  • Pro-People Youth Of Los Angeles: A revolutionary Filipino nationalist youth movement led by Buck Taylor.
  • SignalFire: An informational waterpool of the Maoist movement in general.
  • Revolutionary Anti-Imperialist Movement: The central Maoist Third-Worldist organization of today.
  • RAIM Denver, RAIM Seattle
  • Anti-Imperialism: (Third-Worldist) Analysis of struggles in the global north.
  • Systemic Capital: (Third-Worldist) Analysis of struggles in the global south.
  • Revolutionary Ecology: Environmentalism from a Maoist Third-Worldist perspective.
  • Revolutionary Cascadian Communist Movement: The movement for an independent, socialist Cascadia!
  • The Weekly Bolshevik: Revolutionary, anti-imperialist calendar of events in Occupied North AmeriKKKa.
  • Spectre Of Communism: Forum for Maoist Third-Worldist discussion.
  • Maoism Thirld-Worldism News Wire: Uniting all Third-Worldist revolutionary and national liberation struggles.
  • Onkwehón:we Rising: Indigenous perspective on Third-Worldism and revolution.
  • Unsettling Amerika: For decolonization, in theory and practice.
  • Ancestral Pride: For Indigeneous reoccupation of land and national independence.
  • WARRIOR Publications: Promoting the warrior spirit, and the culture of resistance.
  • Anishinabek Confederacy To Invoke Our Nationhood: Spiritual and revolutionary Onkwehón:we nationalists.
  • New Afrikan Independence Movement
  • Provisional Government Of The Republic Of New Afrika: The government-in-exile of the New Afrikan nation.
  • New Afrikan People’s Organization, New Afrikan Maoist Party, New Afrikan People’s Liberation Army
  • Uhuru Movement: The central website of the Afrikan People’s Socialist Party.
  • The People’s Vanguard: An organization advancing the Black Power movement by building the culture of resistance.
  • New Generation Black Panther Party For Self-Defence: An organization advancing the Black Power movement.
  • Popular Front For The Liberation of Palestine: The revolutionary Anti-Zionist movement for a socialist Palestine.
  • The Socialist Gun Review: An organization devoted to arming and training the revolutionary proletarian.
  • Socialist Economic Bulletin: A journal for (Third-Worldist) Marxist economic analysis.
  • Kersplebedeb: A manufacturer of radical (Third-Worldist) agitprop.
  • Kersplebedeb - Left-Wing Books: Radical (Thirld-Worldist) publishing house.
  • Daily Affirmations For The Revolutionary Proletarian Militant: For combating liberalism and building solidarity!
  • Index Of Educational Resources: Info on class, race, gender, transgender, sexuality, disability, and more.
  • Revolution, Then Roses: A comrade’s personal blog.
  • Shubel Morgan: A prominent MTW. Once he joined LLCO, he ceased to be.
  • Shubel Morgan - YouTube Channel: Videos made by Shubel Morgan.
